💡 Exam Tips

🎯The Goethe A1 exam tests listening, reading, writing, and speaking. You need 60% overall to pass, but each section can be below that.
🎧In the real Hören exam, each audio is played twice. Use the transcript practice here to train for content recognition.
📖Lesen Teil 1 texts are short (emails, notes). Read carefully — one word can change the meaning from Richtig to Falsch.
✍️Schreiben Teil 2 is graded on covering all three points, not perfect grammar. Write simply and clearly.
🗣Sprechen Teil 1 uses the cards from Teil 3 practice. Name the item and form a question: "Können Sie mir bitte … geben?"
